About the map
it is a plastic map of Bukovel which can be used as a reusable ticket, subject to the replenishment of her days skiing and rental equipment. The map gives you the following discounts:
  • 60% for skiing and equipment hire when funding until 31 August;
  • 50% on riding and equipment rental at the completion until 30 November;
  • 20% on riding and equipment rental at the completion from the 1st December until the end of the season (the ski pass valid until complete use).
  • 20% for accommodation in hotels "Bukovel", "Tavel", "iris", "Shelter";
  • 15% in the network of restaurants "Carte.L": Lucky Bull, Filvarok (5 institutions), PLOV, Loungezone Veranda, Ryba Grill, Osteria Italiana, REBRA BBQ, Whisky Bar, SALO, MANGALICA, KOLYBA;
  • 10% on all services of medical health center "Bukovel".
Should know
  • a Discount card is programmed for days of skiing and equipment rentals, which are valid until complete use throughout the winter season, except for the period;
  • for hire of equipment provided for the use of one adult and one child's position in day;
  • the club card is personalized and cannot be used by others. Personal data of the owner stored in the database Bukovel;
  • additional (promotional) discounts are not cumulative;
  • the card expiry is 3 years from the date of purchase. After renewal (renewal) for 50% of the cost of the card, the expiration date shall be extended for the next three years.
